設定R3:

    Router(config)#access-list 1 deny host 2.2.2.2
    Router(config)#access-list 1 permit any
    Router(config)#int e0/0
    Router(config-if)#ip access-group 1 in

在R1測試:

    Router(config)#do ping 3.3.3.3 source 2.2.2.2
